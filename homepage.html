AgriConnect is a technology-driven agricultural supply chain platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers, dealers, customers, and service providers. By integrating digital marketplaces, logistics, storage, and equipment rental services, the platform ensures transparency, efficiency, and fair trade across the entire agricultural ecosystem.
